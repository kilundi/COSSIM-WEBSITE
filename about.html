About us

Cossim Limited is a Last Mile Logistics Company

Cossim Limited is a Last Mile Logistics Company, incorporated in 2017 under the companies act of 2015. Over time, we have managed to steadily grow to become the preferred last mile logistics company.

  • Optimizing Last Mile solutions is at the core of all what we do.

  • We accelerate global trade by delivering products and goods that drive commerce .

  • By focusing on specific needsof each last milelogistics, we strive to delivercreative last mile solutions that help our customers grow business, outpace competitors, and exceed expactations.

Deliver creative Last Mile Solutions that connect people,businesses and communities.
